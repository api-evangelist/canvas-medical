Canvas Medical expanded its support for SMART on FHIR applications, making it easier to embed standardized healthcare apps into the Canvas EMR. It offers a new guide, a FHIR API with 37 resources, and an example app on GitHub, and emphasizes offering more writable FHIR endpoints than competitors for bidirectional data flow.
