Canvas Medical earned top ranking in the 2026 Best in KLAS Awards for ambulatory specialty EHR, based on customer feedback across cardiology, behavioral health, psychiatry, gastroenterology, and primary care, and also received an A rating in the KLAS Relationship category. Customers cited its API-first, highly customizable platform; CEO Adam Farren called it an award authored by customers.
