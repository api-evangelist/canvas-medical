Canvas Medical sponsored XPC's second virtual hackathon, where over 100 clinicians, researchers, and engineers collaborated over a 56-hour weekend. Participants used the Canvas EMR platform, including its SDK, 40+ FHIR API endpoints, and integrated partner tools, to create more than a dozen projects spanning pediatric care to epidemic tracking.
