Two registered dietitians at Vineyard, a virtual GLP-1 weight management clinic, used Canvas Studio to build custom tools in a single afternoon workshop. Summer Kessel created a provider productivity dashboard and Amber Cross built a weight visualization tool mapping patient medication timelines against weight loss data.
